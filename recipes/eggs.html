Cheesy Eggs

Description:

Make savory Scrambled Eggs with Cheese, and Corned Beef Hash - A satisfying meal

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Minced Garlic
  • Chives
  • American Cheese slices

Steps:

  1. Pour 1-3 ounces of milk in a bowl
  2. Break egg shells with a fork and empty entire egg minus the shell into bowl
  3. Repeat once or twice for a total of three eggs!
  4. Whisk with a fork or whisker until eggs are properly beaten
  5. Cut a tablespoon of butter from stick
  6. Turn on stove to medium low and put tablespoon of butter onto pan
  7. Melt the butter and spread it evenly
  8. Add salt and pepper into bowl and empty the bowl onto the stove and wait until semi-solid
  9. Cook and scramble until properly cooked
  10. Add chives, minced garlic, and cheese on top and mix again
  11. Done - empty onto plate - turn off stove
  12. Optional - add corned beef hash on the side from Mary's Kitchen
  13. For Corned Beef Hash cook on medium high on small stove pan, no oil needed, stir as necessary until crispy all around, turn off stove
