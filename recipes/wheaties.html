Wheaties

Description:

Make the perfect bowl of wheaties. This American Classic is the "Breakfast of Champions." The cereal is wheat, hence the name.

Ingredients:

  • Wheaties
  • Milk - your choice
  • Optional: sliced strawberries or bananas

Steps:

  1. Pour Wheaties into bowl.
  2. Pour Milk on top of Wheaties
  3. Optional - add sliced bananas or strawberries on top
