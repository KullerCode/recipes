Cheerios

Description:

Make the perfect bowl of Cheerios. This combination of cereal and milk is a classic. Cheerios is oat cereal.

Ingredients:

  • Cheerios
  • Milk - your choice
  • Optional: sliced strawberries or bananas

Steps:

  1. Pour Cheerios into bowl.
  2. Pour Milk on top of Cheerios
  3. Optional - add sliced bananas or strawberries on top
